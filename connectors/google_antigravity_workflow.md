# Google Antigravity Workflow

1. Clone the repository locally.
2. Open it in Google Antigravity or a compatible local development workflow.
3. Use your own Google account and plan.
4. Keep generated outputs local unless you intentionally promote them to Drive/RAG.

The repository does not store Google credentials and does not require a Google backend for local validation.
